Quick Chapters for YouTube

Quickly make chapters for YouTube videos. Just pause and add a chapter.

On Desktop you can use the following handy commands :
  • Enter as a substitute for "Load Video" and "Add this chapter" buttons.
    For when you have the "Write a title" box selected :
  • Shift to play/pause.
  • Alt go forward by 5 seconds
  • Ctrl go backward by 5 seconds
  • ArrowUp go forward by 30 seconds
  • ArrowDown go backward by 30 seconds
